Firm Overview

Gallenberg PC (also known as Gallenberg Law) is a Los Angeles, California employment law firm led by attorney Rosa Vigil-Gallenberg. The firm represents employees only — never employers — in workplace legal disputes throughout Los Angeles County.

The firm operates on a contingency fee basis: clients pay no upfront fees and no hourly billing. The firm only collects a fee if it recovers money for the client. All initial phone intakes and online case reviews are free.

Practice Areas

The firm focuses exclusively on California employment law, representing employees in:

  • Sexual Harassment — quid pro quo, hostile work environment, unwanted touching, retaliatory treatment after reporting
  • Wrongful Termination — firings in violation of public policy, statute, or contract
  • Workplace Retaliation — adverse action after protected activity (whistleblowing, complaints, leave)
  • Workplace Discrimination — gender, race, age, disability, pregnancy, religion, sexual orientation, national origin
  • Whistleblower Claims — California Labor Code §1102.5 and federal equivalents
  • Wage & Hour Violations — unpaid overtime, missed meal/rest breaks, employee misclassification, off-the-clock work
  • Employment Class Actions — representative and collective actions for groups of employees
