Common Questions

Los Angeles Employment Law FAQ

How much does it cost to hire an employment lawyer in Los Angeles?
At Gallenberg PC, we represent employees on a contingency fee basis. That means there is no upfront cost and no hourly billing — we only get paid if we recover money for you. The initial phone intake and online case review are free.
How long do I have to file a sexual harassment or wrongful termination claim in California?
Deadlines (statutes of limitations) vary by claim. Under California's FEHA, you generally have 3 years from the most recent act to file a complaint with the Civil Rights Department. Waiting can permanently bar your case — contact an attorney as soon as possible.
Do I need to have been fired to bring a claim?
No. You can bring harassment, discrimination, and retaliation claims while still employed. We also handle severance and separation agreement negotiations.
